About Me

Aspiring web developer currently attending University of Kansas' coding bootcamp in an effort to sharpen my skills and hit the ground running in my coding career.

The more I code, the more I realise how great a path it is for me; the combination of creativity, logic and freedom to build leads me to wonder why I didn't start sooner.

Origin Story

I'm a bit of a traveller, having spent time living around the U.K. and Germany. Having Welsh/German parents will do that.

My current home near Kansas City allows me to indulge properly in one of my main hobbies: sports. An avid Eagles fan, and a serial sportsman, there's nothing I won't watch or try.


Reading is another key interest of mine, which has led me to write both in my free time and professionally to some capacity.

I haven't quite reached the loft heights of my favorite authors such as Stephen King or Haruki Murakami, but I tend to write more code than prose nowadays.
