About Me

I'm an aspiring web developer recently graduated from University of Kansas' coding bootcamp in an effort to sharpen my skills and hit the ground running in my coding career.

The more I code, the more I realise how great a path it is for me; the combination of creativity, logic and freedom to build leads me to wonder why I didn't start sooner.

Origin Story

I'm a bit of a traveller, having spent time living around the US, UK and Germany. I love being able to experience living in different environments and cultures.

My current home in Guildford, Surrey, gives me plenty of opportunity to practice one of my main hobbies: sports. An avid Philadelphia Eagles fan, and a serial sportsman, there's nothing I won't watch or try.


Reading is another key interest of mine, which has led me to write both in my free time and professionally to some capacity.

I haven't quite reached the loft heights of my favorite authors such as Stephen King or Haruki Murakami, but I tend to write more code than prose nowadays.
